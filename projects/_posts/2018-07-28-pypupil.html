SUMMARY


I devised an eye-tracker setup and developed the helper module for pupil-labs products in Python (published on PyPI and Github) as a part of the near-eye display project. To reduce the form factor of the near-eye display, We implemented our own calibration without the World Camera of Eye tracker, which is eliminated to reduce the form-factor. To calibrate eye-tracker data, we collect some data before the main measurements based on prepared target and then process it with K-mean clustering and affine transform.
