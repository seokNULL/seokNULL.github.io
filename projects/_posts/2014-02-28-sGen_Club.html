White Cane

A walking aids iOS application using GPS for the visually impaired people.

SUMMARY


We developed a walking aids iOS application using GPS for the visually impaired people. Conventional walking aids applications such as navigation apps are not appropriate for them because they have complex UI such as a keyboard or buttons which is not available for them. We made a gesture/landmark-based service with simple UI and UX by reflecting the walking method of the visually impaired.

* Won bronze and popular awards at the year-end project awards of whole sGen Club teams.

ADDITIONAL MATERIAL


Difference of conventional apps and White Cane
Conventional walking aids app (Left) : many buttons, keyboards and complex UI which are unavailable for visually impaired people.
White Cane (Right) : Simple UI and UX, usable by visually impaired people.

User Interface

Gestures : touch, drag, swipe, shaking
